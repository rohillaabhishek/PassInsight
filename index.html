What is Entropy?

Entropy measures the mathematical randomness of your password in "bits". Higher entropy means more possible combinations. A score above 50 bits is generally considered strong.

Why It Matters

Cyber attacks rely on automated tools testing millions of passwords per second. A truly strong password serves as a mathematical barrier that makes brute-force attacks impossible.
